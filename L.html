Ingredients For Pancakes

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 3.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Combine dry ingredients
  2. Combine wet ingredients
  3. Mix batter
  4. Heat pan
  5. Cook: Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake
  6. Flip and cook each side for about 1-2 minutes
