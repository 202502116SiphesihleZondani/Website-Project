About Us!

Hey, fellow foodies! We are a team of students who are passionate about making cooking easy, affordable, and manageable for students like us. As students, we know how tough it can be to cook meals on a budget, especially when you're juggling classes,assignments, homesickness, and a social life. That is why we created What's Cooking?—a website that is all about sharing delicious, easy-to-make recipes that won't break your budget.

To make cooking easy and enjoyable for us students, while promoting meals we consistently make and reducing food waste, we have built a platform designed especially for you. It is a place where you can quickly search for affordable meal options, and explore recipes that might suit your lifestyle.

But we are more than just a food finder. What's Cooking? is a community where students can share ideas, swap cooking tips, and learn how to make the most out of what you have. Whether you are a beginner learning to cook for the first time, or a foodie experimenting with new flavours, our mission is to help students cook smarter, eat healthier, and enjoy the process of creating something delicious. This is because food is not just fuel, it is an experience.

So, what's cooking today? With us, the answer is always something exciting, something fresh, and something made just for students like you and us.
